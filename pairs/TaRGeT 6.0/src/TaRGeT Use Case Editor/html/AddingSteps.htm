Adding Steps

 

There are two manners to add a step to a flow:

 

  1. Insert a step bellow – it can be done in two ways:
    1. Select a step with double click and after type ENTER
    2. Select a step with double click and click on it with the mouse right button. A context menu will appear. Select the option “Insert a step bellow” in it.

 

  1. Insert a step above
    1. Select a step with double click and click on it with the mouse right button. A context menu will appear. Select the option “Insert a step above” in it.
